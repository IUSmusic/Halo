Halo

A camera-based surface keyboard that can be placed with one hand, rotated, pinned on a 3D-looking plane, and typed with a press gesture instead of only hover timing.

One-hand sculpt mode uses pinch to grab the ghost keyboard, move it, rotate with wrist angle, and resize using pinch opening. Rectangle mode remains available when you want explicit tracing.

How to use

  1. Start the camera and choose your camera source.
  2. Pick One-hand sculpt to place the keyboard with one hand, or Rectangle trace to trace it manually.
  3. In sculpt mode, pinch to grab the keyboard, move your hand to place it, open or close the pinch slightly to resize it, and rotate with your wrist.
  4. Press Pin keyboard when the placement feels right.
  5. Type with any fingertips. In Press gesture mode, a short poke motion triggers the key instead of simple hover timing.

The 3D surface mode is still an in-browser approximation. It uses hand orientation and depth to give the keyboard a surface-like plane and press feel, but it is not full AR plane detection.
